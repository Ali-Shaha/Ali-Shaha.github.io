Elcogen has introduced the generation 2 version of its E3000 stack. The generation 1 elcoStack® E3000 (E3000 G1) and generation 2 elcoStack® E3000 both combine 119 elcoCells®, providing reversible operation functioning both as a fuel cell and electrolysis stack.
